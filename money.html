Tips

Save about three months of rent.

If you can't buy something twice, you can't afford it once.

Keep track of your spending.

If your goal is to make a lot of money, your biggest purchases should be investments.

Keep all receipts.

If you plan to go to college, start saving or you'll have to pay off a lot student loans.

#1 tax preparation software

Build (and maintain) good credit score

Why?

You're able to get a larger loan at a lower interest rate.

They check your credit score when you make a big purchase e.g. houses, cars, rent, etc.

How?

Pay off your credit cards every month, pay back more than the minimum requirement as interest is compounded every month.

Don't be late on paying bills.

Only use credit cards as convenience or when necessary.
