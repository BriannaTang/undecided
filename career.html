Looking for a job as a teen?

Finding jobs as a teen is hard, especially because most sites are for adults and expect you to have highschool and/or higher education completed. Here we found websites that are highschool friendly making your search easier.

Scroll down to find more tips, companies and websites for you to browse.

1. FastWeb

FastWeb, is an incrediable tool for highschool/undergraduate students. You will find everything including internships, scholarships, part-time jobs, and volenteer opertunities. This website is specifically made for you and gives you personalized input. If you are looking for an easy place to find quick jobs, click on this website, it will make your life a whole lot easier.

2. Indeed

Indeed is a website that gives you personalized results for jobs both part-time and full-time. This website has company "profiles" so you can see an average salary and wage as well as the company's rating. This website is usefull if you doing a quick job search such as being a barista or clerk, of course there are others jobs as well.

3. Glassdoor

Glassdoor is a website for both employees and employeers, on this website you will find even more personalized jobs from small, local companies as well as large, well known companies because they all use glassdoor to find prospective applicants like yourself.

4. Chegg

Chegg is an educational service that offers homework help, online tutoring, scholarships, internships, college application advice, textbook rentals, and full/part time job offers that is easy to use and understand. You can find jobs from around the world, which allows you to explore other countries.
