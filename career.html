Looking for a job as a teen?

Finding jobs as a teen is hard, especially because most sites are for adults and are expecting highschool and higher education completed. Here we found websites that are highschool friendly making your search easier.

Scroll down to find more tips, companies and websites for you to browse.

1. FastWeb

FastWeb, by far, is an incrediable tool for highschool/undergraduate students. You will find everything including internships, part-time, and volenteer opertunities. This website is specifically made for you and gives you personalied imput. If you are looking for an easy place to find quick jobs click on this website, it will make your life easier.

2. Indeed

Indeed is a website that gived you personalized results for jobs both part-time and full-time. This website has company "profiles" so you can see average salary and wage as well as rating. Go here is you are looking for a quick job such as barista or clerk, of course there are others jobs as well.

3. Glassdoor

Glassdoor is a website for both employees and employeers, you will find more personalized jobs and from smaller local companies as well because they come to glassdoor to find people they need.

4. Chegg

Chegg has university requruting as well as full/part-time jobs. It's an easy website to use and understand. Jobs here are world wide, so you will find jobs in other countries if that interests you.
